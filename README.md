# Wavey: Real-Time Audio Visualization

**Wavey** is an immersive digital playground designed to transform sound into captivating visual experiences. By bridging the gap between acoustics and art, Wavey provides a real-time window into the hidden world of sound waves, turning your microphone input into a dynamic, shifting landscape of light and geometry.

Built for creative thinkers, musicians, and educators alike, Wavey offers a suite of intuitive controls that allow you to sculpt your sound’s visual identity. Whether you prefer the organic flow of a **circular pulse**, the rhythmic structure of **frequency bars**, or the sharp precision of a **waveform line**, the app gives you the power to toggle styles instantly. Deep customization options allow users to experiment with solid or gradient color schemes, adjust line thickness for visual weight, and scale the output to fit any screen or creative project.
<img width="696" height="651" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/309558f6-c43a-4d92-b6b7-3d408dd0c52b" />

Beyond its aesthetic appeal, Wavey serves as a functional tool for audio monitoring. With its responsive "shape mutation" engine, users can visualize the nuances of their voice or ambient environment, making it an excellent resource for teaching basic physics of sound or simply adding a professional visual layer to live streams and digital performances. 
<img width="1036" height="541" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b62b8255-9dbd-4003-a33e-1aeb93f6955a" />

Lightweight and accessible through a web browser, Wavey removes the barrier between complex audio processing and artistic expression. It is more than just a visualizer; it is an invitation to see the music you hear and the words you speak in an entirely new light.
